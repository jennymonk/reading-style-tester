Reading Comfort Tester

Adjust the controls below while reading the paragraph. Choose what feels easiest for your eyes.

Reading is a highly individual experience. The way text is presented can affect how comfortable it feels to read for long periods. Some people prefer wider spacing between lines, while others find that certain fonts are easier to follow. Background colour and contrast can also influence visual comfort. This small tester allows you to experiment with different settings to see which combination feels easiest for your own eyes. There is no single correct answer. The goal is simply to discover the reading style that feels calm, clear and comfortable for you.
