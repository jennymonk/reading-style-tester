Reading View

A larger, document-style view for trying out your settings
Reading is a highly individual experience. The way text is presented can influence how comfortable it feels to read for extended periods. Some readers prefer wider spacing between lines, while others find certain fonts easier to follow. Background colour and contrast can also affect visual comfort. This page is designed to help you view text in a more realistic, full-page format so you can judge how it feels over time.
Reading Comfort Lab was created to help people explore their own reading preferences in a simple, practical way.
